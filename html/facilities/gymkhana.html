Introduction

The Science Association committee was established in the Anandibai Pradhan Science College in the year 1992. Science Association is a committee established for the students so that they can explore their ideas of science in today's scientific world. There is a huge co-relation between science and life. Students must understand this co-relation and should develop their knowledge in this field.
