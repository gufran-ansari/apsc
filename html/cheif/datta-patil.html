Founder & Former President

Late. Adv. Dattatrey Narayan Patil

Personal Information

Date Of Birth:

4th April 1926

Birth Place:

Roha

Education:

B.A. L.L.B

Profession:

Advocate

Hobby:

Reading

Date of Death:

27th August 2011

Nationality:

Indian

Address:

Thikrul Naka, Alibag, Raigad, Maharashtra-India, 402201

Family Background

Father
  • Narayan Nagu Patil
  • He was member of Legislative Council of Mumbai State.
  • Founder of 'Peasants & Workers Party' (Shetkari Kamgar Paksh) in Maharashtra.
  • Struggled for the upliftment of farmers, workers, and socially deprived peoples.
  • He has played a very important role in the Foundation of Konkan Education Society.
Mother
  • Lakshmibai Narayan Patil.
  • She was Vice-Principal of school
  • worked as teacher for many years.
Profession:- Advocate
  • Started profession on 18th April 1950.
  • He was felicitated by Raigad Bar Association on the occasion of completion of 50 years as Advocate
  • Well known in Maharashtra as a very expert Advocate as a Criminal lawyer.
Political Contributions
  • Senior leader of 'Shetkari Kamgar Paksh'
  • Member of Legislative Assembly of Maharashtra (Total 27- Years) elected as MLA from Alibag Constituency in the year 1957,1967,1978,1980 ,1985 & 1990.
  • He was 'Leader of Opposition Party' of Legislative Assembly of Maharashtra in the year 1987 & 1989.
  • Member of 'Lok lekha Samiti'
  • Member of 'Panchayat Raj Samiti'
  • He has faced jail for 9 months for the Participation in Maharashtra -Karnataka - border issue.
Educational Contributions
  • President:- Konkan Education Society - one of the oldest and largest educational society in Konkan Region and has been awarded with 'Adarsh Shikshan Sanstha' Purskar by Government of Maharashtra under his dynamic leadership.
  • Chairman:- Janta Shikshan Mandal Alibag- Raigad.
  • President:- Maharashtra State Homeopathic Medical College Foundation.
  • Many schools, Junior colleges, senior colleges, Engineering, Medical and Law colleges are started in Raigad, Thane and Sidhudurg districts under his leadership.
Honorary Positions Held
  • President:- Kulaba Maternity Hospital Alibag.
  • President:- Alibag Agricultural Products and Market Committee.
  • President:- Raigad Bazar, Alibag.
Awards
  • First Balasaheb Arekar Awards (Balasaheb Arekar Purskar) from Balasaheb Arekar Trust, Karjat for his educational, social, cultural, and political contributions.
